Click links below for some ride suggestions. If you are a Garmin user, upload a ride… or email us your suggested ride!

Road – Pier, Walua, Napoopoo, Honaunau
Ride south from Kona up to the town of Captain Cook then down to Kealakekua Bay then back.
Time: ~3:15 Hours
Distance: ~47.4 Miles
Elevation Gain: ~3500 Feet

Road – Lako St, Walua Rd, KamIII, Alii Dr
Quick climb up to roughly 1000 feet, down then up another gradual climb. Finish the last 6 miles along Kona’s famed Ali’i Drive.
Time: ~1:30 Hours
Distance: ~20.5 Miles
Elevation Gain: ~2000 Feet

Road – Hualalai Rd, Holualoa, Poi Factory Rd.
Moderately steep climb up to Kona’s Coffee growing country with amazing views of the ocean.
Time: ~2:15 Hours
Distance: ~31.25 Miles
Elevation Gain: ~2500 Feet

Road – Puna Triangle Ride
Beautiful relatively flat loop ride on the far eastern side of the Big Island.Road – Puna Triangle Ride.
Time: ~2:00 Hours
Distance: ~40 Miles
Elevation Gain: ~2000 Feet

Road – Waimea, Old Mamalahoa, Waipio Valley
A Beautiful out and back Ride west of Waimea that includes the Old Mamalahoa Hwy and views of north coast and the famed Waipio Valley.
Time: ~3:30 Hours
Distance: ~50 Miles
Elevation Gain: ~3350 Feet
