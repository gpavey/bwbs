Tips for the Best Bike Assembly:

In order to re-assemble your bicycle to your original fit, our mechanics need to know how you have it set up. Please mark, or have your local mechanic mark all heights and angles. (Tape or marked tape. Whiteout works well on black handlebars and aerobars. Black marker on silver parts. Stem spacers can be taped in their proper place.)
